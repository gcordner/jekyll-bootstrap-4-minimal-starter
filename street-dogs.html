STATE OF GRACE - MAKING OF: - 2008

The Street Dogs

When Hellcat Records told THE STREET DOGS to make a new album, Gabe Bartalos got the call to document it. From rehersal, tracking and to the final mix, this is a fascinating and unflinching look into the real guts of record making through the optics of one of this generations best punk bands.

"Hey you, do you remember when, we were two angry kids"
